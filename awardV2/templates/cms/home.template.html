Caravan and RV Parts and Accessories

Stocking the top-of-the-line brands like Camec, Coast to Coast, Aussie Traveller, Century batteries, AL-KO parts, REDARC electronics, NARVA lighting and electrical, and Thetford caravan appliances and accessories - Award RV has everything you need to fit out your caravan, RV, or 4x4 from top to bottom, front to back, inside and out.

Whether it’s a weekend away or you’re doing the Big Lap, Award RV has the right parts and accessories for your caravan so you can travel in comfort.

Camping Accessories

As Australia’s largest camping accessory store, we can set you up with all the good stuff you’ll want for your next camping trip, with more tents and accessories than anywhere else in the country.

Our massive range is much more than just tents and swags, we’ve got sleeping bags, portable lighting, camp kitchen accessories, water heaters, camp toilets, and plenty of advice if you aren’t sure what you’ll need.

4WD and Car Accessories

Kitting out your four-by is easier than ever with Award RV, we’ve got parts and accessories to get you trekking out in the bush, and then back again if you get yourself stuck. From compressors and UHF radios to recovery & repair equipment and awnings, we’ll help you get where you want to go.

Workshop and Installs

Award RV’s fully equipped workshop can take care of everything – from massive Victron Energy off-grid installations and full wire-ups to Girard hot water heater upgrades, Dometic and Fiamma awning installs, and AUFocus diesel heater fitments. Our experienced technicians ensure every part is installed to the highest standard, letting you drive off with confidence for your next adventure.
